Upgrade iPhone 5s to 10.3.3 from any older iOS without blobs

This quick tutorial explains how to upgrade a7 devices to 10.3.3

NOTE: IF YOU HAVE BLOBS FOR 10.3.3 USE FUTURERESTORE INSTEAD OF THIS.

Requirements

A jailbreak

Filza or iFile

Your jailbreak must be semi-untethered.

If you are currently on an untethered jailbreak, follow the instructions below this paragraph.

Procedure

Open Filza and go to /System/Library/CoreServices

Open "SystemVersion.plist" with the built-in plist editor in Filza or with the text editor of iFile.

Replace the current build number with "11D167" and the current version to "7.1"

Now go to /Applications and delete Setup.app - Important: if you skip this step, at the next step, when you have to reboot your device, you would get stuck at activation error and you will be forced to restore.

Reboot the device

Go to Settings - General - Erase all content and data - Confirm

After it finishes, go to Settings - General - Update

iOS 10.3.3 OTA update will show, install it.

When done, put a sim in your device (in case of iPhone) and activate it.

After you done configuring it, Settings - General - Erase all content and data - Confirm

Put the sim again and activate it again

Done.

Method for untethered jailbreaks

Please note: untethered jailbreaks can cause issues during the procedure.

Procedure

Open Filza and go to /System/Library/CoreServices

Open "SystemVersion.plist" with the built-in plist editor in Filza or with the text editor of iFile.

Replace the current build number with "11D167" and the current version to "7.1"

Now go to /Applications and delete Setup.app - Important: if you skip this step, at the next step, when you have to reboot your device, you would get stuck at activation error and you will be forced to restore.

Go to Cydia and remove your untether. For example, on pangu9 jailbreak you should remove Pangu 9.0.x-9.1 untether package.

Reboot the device

Go to Settings - General - Erase all content and data - Confirm

After it finishes, go to Settings - General - Update

iOS 10.3.3 OTA update will show, install it.

When done, put a sim in your device (in case of iPhone) and activate it.

After you done configuring it, Settings - General - Erase all content and data - Confirm

Put the sim again and activate it again

Done.
